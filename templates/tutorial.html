System Manual

Protocol v1.0

Welcome to the SafeZard Training Interface. Review the operational guidelines below to understand navigation, simulation controls, and hazard identification protocols.

01

Command Interface

Navigate the system using the Sidebar Dock on the left. Access the Main Menu for mission selection, Library for archives, and Analytics for performance data. The active module is highlighted in Neon Green.

02

Video Simulation

Immersive video scenarios replicate real-world environments. Observe the surroundings carefully. Controls for Play, Pause, and Replay are located at the bottom of the viewport.

03

Hazard Identification

Critical incidents require immediate input. When a Decision Point appears, the simulation will pause. Select the correct safety protocol from the on-screen options to proceed.

04

Performance Review

Upon mission completion, a detailed Assessment Report is generated. Proficiency scores are logged in the Analytics module. Aim for >80% accuracy to earn certification badges.
